FAQs

To sign up, download the EatsExpress app from the App Store or Google Play, open the app, and follow the on-screen instructions to create an account. You'll need to provide your email address, create a password, and enter some basic information like your address.

Downloading and using the app is free. However, you will be charged for the food you order, and there may be additional delivery fees or service charges depending on your location and the restaurant's policies.

To place an order, open the app, browse through the list of available restaurants, select the items you want to order, and add them to your cart. Once you're ready, proceed to checkout, enter your delivery address, choose a payment method, and confirm your order.

EatsExpres accepts various payment methods including credit/debit cards, digital wallets (like PayPal or Google Pay), and sometimes cash on delivery, depending on your location.

If you've forgotten your password, go to the login page and select the "Forgot Password" option. Follow the instructions to reset your password via email or SMS.
